Colophon

The title typeface is Chunk by Meredith Mandel.

Chunk is an ultra-bold slab serif typeface that is reminiscent of old American Western woodcuts, broadsides, and newspaper headlines. Used mainly for display, the fat block lettering is unreserved yet refined for contemporary use.

It's available through the League of Movable Type under the Open Font License.

The graphic design work was done by Missing Monkey Graphics of Orlando Florida. The Site layout and Design and template programming was done by Tamarou.
